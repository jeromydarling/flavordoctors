The Technology Stack

One codebase, one deploy, no servers. Every request is served from Cloudflare's edge network in 300+ cities.

ComputeCloudflare Workers — a single Worker serves both the customer-facing React application and the entire API, deployed globally in seconds.
DatabaseCloudflare D1 (distributed SQLite) — products, orders, subscriptions, customers, inventory, marketing, and support data with versioned migrations.
StorageCloudflare R2 object storage for all product and lifestyle imagery.
AICloudflare Workers AI — Llama 3.1 for copy, chat, and recipes; Flux-1 for product & lifestyle image generation. No external AI vendor.
PaymentsStripe — checkout, subscriptions, coupons, promotion codes, customer billing portal, and HMAC-verified webhooks.
EmailCloudflare Email Service for transactional and marketing sends (with an automatic fallback provider), CAN-SPAM compliant with one-click unsubscribe.
FrontendReact 18 + Vite + Tailwind CSS, TypeScript end-to-end.
AnalyticsGoogle Analytics 4 — client-side tagging plus server-side purchase events (ad-blocker-proof revenue tracking).
CI/CD & QAGitHub Actions: type-checks, builds, database migrations, and a 78-test Playwright end-to-end suite covering signup through checkout, admin, and automations on every release.

The Customer Experience Front end

  • Themed storefront — the entire shop plays out as a clinic: products are "treatments," the quiz is an "Intake Exam," the account page is "My Chart."
  • Catalog — 34 products across 5 collections (doctored mayos, spiced ghee butters, burger sauces, crunch toppers, seasonings), each with AI-generated "Doctor's Notes" prescription copy and AI-generated product photography.
  • Intake Exam — a 5-question flavor quiz that produces an AI diagnosis and a personalized 3-product prescription, with one click to add all three to cart.
  • The Pharmacist — an AI consult chat grounded in the live catalog that recommends and adds products.
  • Front Desk — an AI support agent that answers order, shipping, and subscription questions from the customer's own data, and hands off to a human ticket when needed.
  • Clinical Trials — limited product drops with launch schedules, stock caps, guest waitlists, and 48-hour early access for subscribers.
  • Smart cart — live bundle-discount and free-shipping nudges, loyalty-point redemption, quantity controls.
  • Reviews & ratings — one-click star ratings on past purchases plus written, moderated reviews shown on product pages.
  • Treatment Plans — an SEO recipe hub: search-indexed recipe pages that each end in a product call-to-action.
  • Back-in-stock alerts — sold-out products collect notify-me signups and email them automatically on restock.
  • Full account self-service — order history, one-click reorder, password reset & change, email preferences, and self-service account deletion.

Commerce & Subscriptions Revenue engine

  • Rx Box subscriptions — three tiers ($39 / $54 / $69 per box at 4 / 6 / 8 items) on monthly, every-2-months, or annual cadence (annual = 2 months free, prepaid cash up front).
  • First box 20% off — automatic for brand-new subscribers.
  • Box customization — subscribers hand-pick every item in their next box.
  • Skip / pause / resume — one click each; billing pauses cleanly through Stripe.
  • Cancel-flow save offers — canceling opens a retention ladder (skip → pause → one-time 20% off next box → cancel at period end with undo). Saves vs. cancels are measured.
  • Pricing plays — any 3+ items 15% off automatically; free shipping over $45; timed sitewide promotions with countdown banner and Stripe promo codes.
  • Guest checkout — no account required to buy; guests are folded into the CRM by email.

Loyalty, Referrals & Retention LTV

  • Board Certification loyalty — 1 point per $1 across orders and subscription invoices; four status tiers.
  • Points redemption — points are worth 1¢ each and spend in $5 blocks right in the cart; balances are only debited when payment settles.
  • Refer a Patient — every customer gets a share link; both sides earn $5 in points when the friend's first order is paid. Signups, conversions, and earnings shown on the customer's own card.
  • Refill reminders — automatic "your Rx is running low" emails 30–45 days after purchase.
  • Win-back campaigns — automatic outreach to lapsed subscribers.
  • NPS pulse — a one-question survey a week after delivery; unhappy scores automatically open a support ticket, happy ones are routed to reviews and referrals. The live NPS score sits on the analytics dashboard.

The Back Office Admin & staff

  • Role-based access — admin and support-rep roles; reps see orders, customers, inbox, analytics, and inventory only. Role changes apply instantly, and the owner account can never be locked out.
  • Audit trail — every sensitive action (role changes, campaign sends, promos, product edits, order status changes, points grants, stock movements) is logged with who/what/when.
  • Customer OS — every customer with a computed lifecycle stage (Lead → Customer → Subscriber → VIP / At-risk / Lapsed) and a full file: orders, subscription, points, quiz diagnosis, notes, tickets, email history, reviews. One-off emails and goodwill points from the same screen.
  • Support inbox — ticket queues with complete threads (customer, AI transcript, agent); replies are emailed and mirrored to the customer's account.
  • Product management — full catalog CRUD with AI description and product-image regeneration built in.
  • Order management — status pipeline from paid through delivered/refunded.
  • Analytics dashboard — monthly-equivalent recurring revenue, active subscribers, 30-day and lifetime revenue, average order value, repeat-purchase rate, top products, list growth, NPS, cancel-flow saves — plus a distributor-readiness scorecard tracking retail-launch benchmarks.

Inventory & Operations Supply chain

  • Lot-level tracking — every co-packer delivery is recorded as a lot with code, quantity, best-by date, and PO reference.
  • FEFO fulfillment — orders and subscription boxes consume the earliest-expiring lot first, so any jar can be traced to its batch (food-safety ready).
  • Committed-stock forecasting — the system knows the contents of every upcoming subscription box, so available stock = on-hand minus next month's box demand, before it bills.
  • Reorder alerts — a weekly email flags every SKU at or below its reorder point.
  • Movement ledger — receipts, sales, box shipments, and cycle-count adjustments in one auditable stream.

Marketing OS Growth engine

  • Email campaigns — audience segments (all, leads, customers, VIPs, lapsed…), A/B subject-line testing, open and click tracking, consent-checked sends with compliant footers.
  • Lifecycle flows — editable automated sequences (welcome drips, post-purchase follow-ups) that run themselves nightly.
  • Landing page builder — server-rendered campaign pages with email capture and per-page signup counts.
  • Promotions — create a sale once; the site banner, countdown timer, and Stripe promo code all deploy together.
  • AI Content Studio — one-click weekly social calendars, TikTok captions, email subject lines, Flux lifestyle imagery, and Treatment Plan recipes (drafted, edited, and published in-app).
  • B2B kit — print-ready sell sheets per collection and a RangeMe-formatted CSV with wholesale pricing for retail buyers.
  • Contact CRM — every email address with source attribution, UTM capture, consent state, and CSV export.

Search & Discoverability SEO

  • Edge-injected SEO — per-page titles, descriptions, canonicals, and social-share cards rendered at the CDN edge for full crawler visibility.
  • Structured data — Product (with live review ratings), Organization, WebSite, and Recipe schema for rich search results.
  • Dynamic sitemap & robots — every product and recipe page, always current.
  • llms.txt — a machine-readable catalog map so AI answer engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity) recommend the brand accurately.
  • Canonical domain enforcement — one indexed origin, permanent redirects from all aliases.

Security & Quality

  • Payments integrity — cryptographically verified Stripe webhooks; loyalty and inventory operations are idempotent, so retries and replays can never double-charge, double-credit, or double-ship.
  • Account security — salted password hashing, signed session tokens, hashed single-use password-reset links, anti-probing responses.
  • Least privilege — role-based API enforcement (not just hidden buttons) with live revocation and a permanent audit log.
  • Test coverage — 78 automated end-to-end browser tests run on every change: signup → quiz → cart → checkout → subscription → support → admin → inventory → referrals.
  • Zero-downtime releases — every merge type-checks, tests, migrates the database, and deploys globally, automatically.
